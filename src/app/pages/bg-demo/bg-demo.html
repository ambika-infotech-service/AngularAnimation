Background Animations

CSS Background Components

Pure-CSS animated layers — each component is self-contained and composable. Combine multiple layers inside any position: relative container.

Individual Components

app-floating-shapes

Floating Shapes

Two large blurred orbs that drift slowly using a figure-eight path.

app-hero-particles

Hero Particles

Six floating micro-dots rising upward with a looping SVG wave at the base.

app-hero-shimmer

Hero Shimmer

A diagonal light sweep that passes across the surface at a slow, continuous pace.

app-geometric-shapes

Geometric Shapes

Rotating square, bobbing triangle, spinning hexagon, and pulsing circle in cyan.

app-glow-nodes

Glow Nodes

Five glowing cyan dots scattered across the surface, pulsing in and out.

app-light-rays

Light Rays

Six thin rays radiating from the centre and slowly rotating as a unit.

app-depth-layers

Depth Layers

Three parallax planes of white micro-dots, each drifting at a different speed.

app-starfield-bg

Starfield

80 twinkling stars with randomised size and timing, plus three shooting stars.

app-aurora-bg

Aurora

Four translucent colour bands drifting and skewing to mimic the northern lights.

app-neon-grid-bg

Neon Grid

Cyberpunk-style grid with a descending scanline, flicker stripe, and pulse dots.

app-bubbles-bg

Bubbles

18 translucent spheres of varied sizes rising from the bottom with a wobble.

Combination Scenes

Layer multiple components inside a single container to compose richer backgrounds.
