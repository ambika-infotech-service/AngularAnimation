Background Animations

CSS Background Components

Pure-CSS animated layers — each component is self-contained and composable. Combine multiple layers inside any position: relative container.

@for (filter of filters; track filter.value) { }

Individual Components

app-floating-shapes

Floating Shapes

Two large blurred orbs that drift slowly using a figure-eight path.

app-hero-particles

Hero Particles

Six floating micro-dots rising upward with a looping SVG wave at the base.

app-hero-shimmer

Hero Shimmer

A diagonal light sweep that passes across the surface at a slow, continuous pace.

app-geometric-shapes

Geometric Shapes

Rotating square, bobbing triangle, spinning hexagon, and pulsing circle in cyan.

app-glow-nodes

Glow Nodes

Five glowing cyan dots scattered across the surface, pulsing in and out.

app-light-rays

Light Rays

Six thin rays radiating from the centre and slowly rotating as a unit.

app-depth-layers

Depth Layers

Three parallax planes of white micro-dots, each drifting at a different speed.

app-starfield-bg

Starfield

80 twinkling stars with randomised size and timing, plus three shooting stars.

app-aurora-bg

Aurora

Four translucent colour bands drifting and skewing to mimic the northern lights.

app-neon-grid-bg

Neon Grid

Cyberpunk-style grid with a descending scanline, flicker stripe, and pulse dots.

app-bubbles-bg

Bubbles

18 translucent spheres of varied sizes rising from the bottom with a wobble.

app-mesh-gradient-bg

Mesh Gradient

Three drifting blurred color masses blending like a fluid gradient atmosphere.

app-constellation-bg

Constellation

Blinking star nodes connected by subtle animated links for a sci-fi map look.

app-radar-rings-bg

Radar Rings

Expanding concentric pulses and a rotating sweep cone inspired by radar screens.

app-glitch-stripes-bg

Glitch Stripes

Retro scanlines with moving glitch bands for a subtle digital interference effect.

app-prism-caustics-bg

Prism Caustics

Layered refracted light fans that rotate and overlap like sunlight bouncing through glass.

app-topographic-flow-bg

Topographic Flow

Animated contour rings and drifting ridges that feel like a living terrain map.

app-vortex-tunnel-bg

Vortex Tunnel

Concentric energy rings and a spinning beam that create a portal-like depth illusion.
